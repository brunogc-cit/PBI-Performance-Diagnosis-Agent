The ADE - Trade semantic model has 45 tables (9 DirectQuery, 29 Dual, 7 Import) with 801 measures across a 6-billion-row daily trade snapshot fact table. The model spans 5 Databricks catalogs (sales, product, technology, supplychain, sourcingandbuying) with all serve-layer models materialised as views. Critical performance issues stem from: (1) a massive DirectQuery fact table (6.05B rows) as the central hub with 21 relationships, (2) 49 critical-complexity DAX measures generating up to 14 estimated SQL subqueries each, (3) 228 measures using ALL/ALLEXCEPT filter clearing patterns that force full re-aggregation, (4) 11 Dual-mode tables that could safely switch to Import, (5) 650 unused column candidates inflating scan width, and (6) 12 report pages averaging 22+ visuals each without query reduction settings enabled. The combination of wide column selection, cross-catalog JOINs, and no date filtering on serve views creates severe scan amplification on every user interaction.
